About Us

The Queen's Park Bangladesh Association (QPBA) is a community-driven organisation dedicated to supporting and empowering the Bangladeshi and other community in Queen's Park and surrounding areas.

Our mission is to promote cultural understanding, provide essential services, and create opportunities for personal and professional development.
